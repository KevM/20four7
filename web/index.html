Designed to Fade into the Background

Simple, focus-oriented tools and features that let you craft your workspace atmosphere or evening lean-back routine.

Tag-Based Guide

Instantly surf streams by mood categories. Swap from firesides to lofi beats in a single tap on the HUD, or hide it for a full-screen ambient view.

Remote Catalog Curation

An automatically updating cloud catalog keeps your TV guide fresh with high-quality, TOS-compliant ambient livestreams without requiring App Store updates.

Sleep Timer

Set a timer before bed or focus blocks. The stream will dim and shut down peacefully to save your battery or device energy.

Add Custom Channels

Found a cozy stream you love? Add your own streams using custom YouTube URLs or handle names, automatically stored locally inside your device using SwiftData.

AirPlay Support

Stream directly to your Apple TV, smart TV, or home speaker system to bring ambient comfort to your entire living room environment.

Complete Privacy

No tracking, no user profiles, and no external tracking databases. Your added channels stay completely local to your iPhone or iPad.
